What is RuneCraft?

RuneCraft is a private, creator-driven Minecraft SMP built for members, viewers, and collaborators. This hub helps you learn about the server, explore our creators, and find the best ways to connect.

  • Creator-focused content and weekly featured talent.
  • Live server status and member activity all in one place.
  • Connect with us via Discord or follow our TikTok for the latest content from RuneCraft.
  • You must be 18+ and a content creator to join RuneCraft.
  • If you apply we may take up to a few months to review your application depending on our current status.

About Us

RuneCraft SMP is a private 18+ creator-focused Minecraft server built for people who love collaboration, storytelling, big builds, and turning in-game moments into content worth sharing.

  • Creator-driven SMP with a focus on community, consistency, and fun.
  • Perfect for builders, survival players, roleplayers, and content creators who want active collaborators.
  • Season 2 is all about fresh builds, better stories, and helping creators grow together.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    FAQ

    What is RuneCraft SMP?

    RuneCraft is a private, creator-driven Minecraft SMP for content creators aged 18+.

    How do I apply?

    Fill out the application form linked below and submit your content for review.

    What are the requirements?

    You must be 18+, a content creator, and willing to follow community guidelines.

    How long does application review take?

    Applications may take up to a few months depending on our current status.
